How to Use
1
Add people — tap "+ Person" in the header. Each person gets their own bar on the chart and their own card below.
2
Add subjects — on any person's card, tap the Subjects button. Add whatever subjects you study, pick a colour for each, and tap Save. Subjects are shared across all people in your session.
3
Log questions — enter how many you did per subject and tap Add. The chart and totals update immediately.
4
Connect a friend — tap ⚡ in the header, copy your Session ID, and send it. Your friend pastes it in "Join a Session" and hits Join. Both boards sync live.
5
Track progress — use the chart views (Today / By Day / Week / All-Time), the calendar, and the Leaderboard tab.
About QTrack

QTrack is a simple study tracking platform designed to help users organise their work, monitor progress, and keep everything in one place. It supports all subjects and is made to work across devices so your data stays connected wherever you use it.

The goal of QTrack is to provide a clean and straightforward experience without unnecessary complexity. You can track completed questions, manage subjects, and maintain a clear record of your progress without relying on scattered notes or multiple apps pretending to "increase productivity" while mainly increasing screen time.

QTrack is independently developed with the help of modern AI tools to improve features, usability, and development speed while keeping the platform simple and user-focused.

⚠️ The app is in development phase and may cause bugs and errors. Apologies from the developer's end in advance.
FAQ
What is a Session ID and how do I use it? ▼
A Session ID is a unique code that links multiple devices to the same shared board. Tap ⚡ in the header, copy your Session ID, and send it to your friend. They tap ⚡, paste the ID into "Join a Session" and hit Join. Both of you will see the same data syncing live every 5 seconds. Your session is remembered — you won't need to re-enter it next time.
Can QTrack track questions automatically? ▼
No — QTrack is a manual tracking tool. You enter how many questions you've completed yourself. This is intentional: automatic tracking would require integration with specific apps or platforms, adding complexity and privacy concerns. The manual approach is fast, simple, and works regardless of where you're studying.
Can QTrack connect with other apps? ▼
Currently, no. QTrack does not integrate with third-party apps. All data lives within QTrack's own Firebase database. Integration is on the long-term roadmap but is not available in this version.
How can my friends join my session? ▼
(1) Tap ⚡ in the header. (2) Copy your Session ID. (3) Send it via WhatsApp or any messaging app. (4) Your friend opens QTrack, taps ⚡, pastes the ID and hits Join. They'll immediately see your board and can add themselves as a person.
