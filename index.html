About QTrack

QTrack is a simple study tracking platform designed to help users organise their work, monitor progress, and keep everything in one place. It supports all subjects and is made to work across devices so your data stays connected wherever you use it.

The goal of QTrack is to provide a clean and straightforward experience without unnecessary complexity. You can track completed questions, manage subjects, and maintain a clear record of your progress without relying on scattered notes or multiple apps pretending to "increase productivity" while mainly increasing screen time.

QTrack is independently developed with the help of modern AI tools to improve features, usability, and development speed while keeping the platform simple and user-focused.

⚠️ The app is in development phase and may cause bugs and errors. Apologies from the developer's end in advance.
FAQ
What is a Session ID and how do I use it? ▼
A Session ID is a unique code that links multiple devices to the same shared board. To use it: tap the ⚡ badge in the top header, copy your Session ID, and send it to your friend via WhatsApp or any messaging app. Your friend opens QTrack, taps ⚡, pastes the ID into "Join a Session" and hits Join. Both of you will now see the same data, syncing live every few seconds.
Can QTrack track questions automatically? ▼
No — QTrack is a manual tracking tool. You enter the number of questions you've completed yourself. This is intentional: automatic tracking would require integration with specific apps or platforms, which adds complexity and privacy concerns. The manual approach keeps it fast, simple, and works regardless of where you're studying — a textbook, a printed sheet, or any app.
Can QTrack connect with other apps? ▼
Currently, no. QTrack does not integrate with any third-party apps like Notion, Google Sheets, or study platforms. All data lives within QTrack's own system. Integration with external tools is on the long-term roadmap but is not available in this version. The focus right now is on getting the core tracking experience right before adding connectors.
How can my friends join my session? ▼
Simple: (1) Open QTrack and tap the ⚡ badge in the top right. (2) Copy your Session ID shown there. (3) Send it to your friend. (4) Your friend opens QTrack on their device, taps ⚡, pastes the Session ID into the "Join a Session" field, and hits Join. They'll immediately see your board and can add themselves as a person. No accounts, no downloads — just the HTML file and the session code.
